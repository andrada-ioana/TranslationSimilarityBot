Snow White (Alba ca Zăpada) is a classic fairy tale character, a princess famed for her beauty, who is the protagonist in stories by the Brothers Grimm and a famous Disney animated film, fleeing her evil stepmother and finding refuge with the Seven Dwarfs, ultimately saved by a prince after a poisoned apple. A new live-action Disney movie starring Rachel Zegler and Gal Gadot is set for release in 2025.
